Japan Travel Planner (May Trip)

10–14 Day Culture + Nature Focused Itinerary

Welcome

This itinerary is designed specifically for Indian travelers visiting Japan for the first time, focusing on low crowds, culture, and nature while minimizing fatigue.

Route: Tokyo → Kamakura → Kyoto → Osaka → Tokyo

Best Feature: Every day has 3 options based on crowd preference.

Day 1 – Arrival Tokyo

🟢 Low Crowd

Arrive in Tokyo → Take Narita Express (~1 hr) → Stay in Asakusa.

Evening: Walk Sumida River, explore quiet streets, visit Senso-ji after 8 PM (empty & magical).

Food: Try local ramen shop (¥1200)

Cost: ₹6,000

Tip: Avoid Shibuya on day 1 to reduce fatigue.

🟡 Balanced

Check-in → Visit Tokyo Skytree → Explore Asakusa market street.

Transport: Metro (~20 min)

🔴 Popular

Shibuya Crossing → Hachiko statue → Shinjuku nightlife.

Tip: Expect heavy crowds.

Day 2 – Tokyo Culture

Morning: Meiji Shrine at 6 AM

Afternoon: Daikanyama (quiet cafes, bookstores)

Evening: Meguro river walk

Cost: ₹5,000

Harajuku → Omotesando → Shibuya evening

DisneySea full day (crowded but unique)

Day 3 – Kamakura Day Trip

Early train (1 hr) → Hokokuji Bamboo Forest → Hasedera Temple → Quiet beach sunset

Cost: ₹4,500

Great Buddha → Komachi Street → Temples

Full tourist circuit

Day 4 – Travel to Kyoto

Shinkansen (2.5 hr) → Gion evening walk → Hidden alleys

Cost: ₹12,000

Kyoto station exploration + Nishiki market

Full tourist Kyoto night walk

Day 5 – Kyoto Temples

Fushimi Inari at 6 AM → hike halfway → avoid crowds

Afternoon: Uji tea town

Kinkaku-ji + Arashiyama bamboo grove

All major Kyoto spots in one day (exhausting)

Day 6 – Kyoto Hidden Gems

Philosopher’s Path → Nanzen-ji → Ginkaku-ji

Arashiyama full exploration

Tour bus Kyoto

Day 7 – Nara or Osaka

Nara early morning → feed deer → quiet temples

Osaka castle + Dotonbori

Universal Studios

Day 8 – Osaka Food + Culture

Local food tour → Shinsekai → hidden bars

Dotonbori + shopping

Theme parks

Day 9 – Return Tokyo

Bullet train → Relaxation → Onsen experience

Shopping Ginza

Akihabara full day

Day 10 – Departure

Relax café morning → airport

Last minute shopping

Rush sightseeing

Where to Stay

Tokyo: Asakusa (budget), Shinjuku (connectivity)

₹4k–₹15k/night

Kyoto: Gion / Kawaramachi

Osaka: Namba area

Tip: Stay near JR stations

Food Guide

Must Try: Sushi, Ramen, Okonomiyaki, Takoyaki

Street Areas: Dotonbori, Nishiki Market

Budget: ₹800–₹2500/day

Flights: ₹50k–₹80k

Stay: ₹40k–₹1L

Food: ₹20k

Transport: ₹25k

Total: ₹1.5L–₹2.5L

Japan Visa (India)

Documents: Passport, photos, bank statement, ITR

Travel Tips

Carry cash (Japan is cash-heavy)

Respect silence in public transport

Pack light jacket (May weather mild)
