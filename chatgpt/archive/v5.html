🇯🇵 Japan Travel Planner

Tokyo Round Trip | Culture • Nature • Smart Travel

🌿 Trip Overview

  • Duration: 10–12 Days
  • Route: Tokyo → Kamakura → Kawaguchiko → Kyoto → Osaka → Tokyo
  • Weather: 15–25°C (Spring, pleasant)

✨ Travel Style

  • Low crowd & hidden gems focus
  • Balanced + popular alternatives available
  • Slow travel to reduce fatigue

🎯 Key Highlights

  • Mt Fuji views (Kawaguchiko)
  • Kyoto temples + villages
  • Osaka street food
  • Optional Theme Park (Universal Studios)
Day 1 – Tokyo Arrival

🟢 Slow Arrival

  • Arrive at Tokyo (Narita/Haneda), complete immigration (~1 hr)
  • Take airport limousine bus/train to hotel (1–1.5 hr)
  • Evening: Explore nearby quiet streets (Daikanyama or local neighborhood)
  • Dinner: Light Japanese meal (ramen or convenience store bento)

🟡 Light Exploration

  • After check-in, visit a nearby café district
  • Walk through local shopping street (non-touristy)
  • Try your first sushi/ramen experience

🔴 Popular Start

  • Visit Shibuya Crossing (evening lights)
  • Optional: Tokyo Tower night view
  • Expect crowds but great first impression of Tokyo
💡 Keep it light to recover from travel fatigue
Day 2 – Tokyo Exploration

🟢 Local Tokyo

  • Start early at Meiji Shrine (peaceful forest shrine, 8 AM)
  • Explore Shimokitazawa – vintage shops, indie cafés
  • Lunch at a small ramen shop (~¥1000)
  • Evening: Quiet walk along Meguro river

🟡 Balanced Tokyo

  • Visit Meiji Shrine + Harajuku street
  • Explore Shinjuku Gyoen park (relaxation spot)
  • Evening: Shinjuku (food + lights)

🔴 Busy Tokyo

  • Akihabara (anime + electronics)
  • Shinjuku nightlife district
  • Expect heavy crowds and long walking hours
🚇 Use metro pass | Cost: ₹6k–₹10k
Day 3 – Kamakura Day Trip

🟢 Nature + Peace

  • Take train (~1 hr from Tokyo)
  • Visit lesser-known temples (Zeniarai Shrine)
  • Walk coastal trail with ocean views
  • Relax at a beach café

🟡 Classic Kamakura

  • Visit Great Buddha (Daibutsu)
  • Explore Hasedera Temple (gardens + views)
  • Lunch: Local seafood or vegetarian tempura

🔴 Tourist Route

  • Main temple circuit + Komachi street shopping
  • Crowded but lively atmosphere
🚆 Round trip ~₹1500 | Great cultural escape from Tokyo
Day 4 – Mt Fuji (Kawaguchiko)

🟢 Scenic Retreat

  • Travel via bus/train (~2.5 hrs)
  • Walk around Lake Kawaguchi (peaceful Fuji views)
  • Stay in Ryokan + Onsen experience
  • Sunset photography by lake

🟡 Balanced

  • Cable car to Mt Fuji viewpoint
  • Lake cruise
  • Visit small museums nearby

🔴 Popular

  • Visit crowded Instagram photo spots
  • Tour bus circuits
🏔 Stay overnight | ₹10k–₹20k
Day 5 – Travel to Kyoto
  • Take Shinkansen (~2.5 hrs)
  • Relax at hotel after check-in
  • Evening walk in quiet Kyoto streets
  • Visit Gion district (traditional streets)
  • Spot geisha (evening)
  • Explore Nishiki market (busy food street)
🚄 Cost ~₹8k–₹12k
Day 6 – Kyoto Temples

🟢 Best Experience

  • Visit Fushimi Inari at 6 AM (empty torii gates)
  • Explore deeper hiking trails beyond main shrine
  • Afternoon rest + café
  • Fushimi Inari (8 AM)
  • Gion streets + tea house
  • Bamboo Grove + Golden Pavilion
  • Expect heavy crowds
⏰ Early start = best experience
Day 7 – Hidden Kyoto
  • Visit Ohara village (rural Kyoto)
  • Explore Sanzen-in temple (nature + silence)
  • Local countryside lunch
  • Philosopher’s Path walk
  • Visit small temples along route
  • Main Kyoto temple circuit
Day 8 – Nature Day
  • Kurama to Kibune hike (mountain trail)
  • Hot spring experience
  • Scenic train ride
  • Light walking trails
  • Kyoto city exploration
Day 9 – Osaka
  • Explore quieter food alleys
  • Try local okonomiyaki
  • Dotonbori street food
  • Shopping in Namba
  • Nightlife + crowded streets
Day 10 – Theme Park / Relax
  • Relax day
  • Café hopping + shopping
  • Visit theme park for half day
  • Full day Universal Studios (Harry Potter World)

🏨 Stay Guide

  • Tokyo: Shinjuku, Shibuya (₹6k–₹15k)
  • Kyoto: Gion, Kawaramachi (₹7k–₹18k)
  • Osaka: Namba (₹6k–₹12k)
  • Ryokan: ₹12k–₹25k (Kawaguchiko)

🍜 Food Guide

  • Ramen, Sushi, Okonomiyaki, Takoyaki
  • Vegetarian: Tempura, Udon, Curry rice
  • Indian restaurants available in all cities

💰 Budget (INR)

  • Flights: ₹50k–₹80k
  • Stay: ₹70k–₹1.5L
  • Food: ₹20k–₹40k
  • Transport: ₹25k–₹40k
  • Total: ₹1.5L – ₹2.5L

🛂 Visa

  • Apply via VFS
  • 5–7 days processing
  • Documents: Passport, ITR, bank statements

💡 Tips

  • Carry cash (Japan still uses it)
  • Respect silence in public
  • Use IC cards (Suica/Pasmo)
  • Get pocket WiFi
